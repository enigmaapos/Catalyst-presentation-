Catalyst at a Glance

Catalyst is a modular NFT staking & governance protocol enabling users to stake ERC-721s for CATA rewards, participate in burn-weighted governance, and rely on a secure Guardian Council recovery mechanism.

Mission
Non-Profit R&D Utility

Immutable fee split 90% Burn / 9% Treasury / 1% Deployer.

Usage
  1. Locate and save your proxy contract address.
  2. Connect your wallet to stake and govern.
  3. Use tabs to stake NFTs, vote, claim rewards, and manage council actions.
