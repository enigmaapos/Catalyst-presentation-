3. Collection Tiers & Mechanics

Verified (Standard)

Custodial staking, normal fees, full privileges.

Unverified (Proof-of-Burn)

Must pay registration + surcharge escrow. Forfeiture is $\mathbf{90/9/1}$ split. Reduced rights until governance-approved upgrade.

Blue-Chip (Non-Custodial)

Optional non-custodial model. Wallet pays one-time enrollment fee for reward access, avoiding custody risk.

4. Governance & Hardening

Burn-Weighted Governance (BWCG)

  • Voting weight scaled by **stake contribution & burn activity**.
  • `collectionVoteCapPercent` prevents single-collection domination.
  • Governs proposals for fee changes, reward adjustments, and Council reseeding.

Security Hardening (CRITICAL)

The generic AccessControl role functions are **DISABLED** in all four contracts and hard-coded to revert:

  • grantRole()
  • revokeRole()
  • renounceRole()

Role changes must only happen through the auditable Governance or Guardian Council proposals.

5. Decentralized Recovery System (DRS)

Batch Guardian Council (BGC)

Dual-batch (Active/Standby) model with **7 Guardians** each, ensuring decentralized recovery without a central point of failure.

Recovery Timelines (Aligned)

1.

Approval Threshold: $\mathbf{5}$ of $\mathbf{7}$ votes required for recovery validity.

2.

Last Honest Veto: $\mathbf{6}$ of $\mathbf{7}$ approval grants a **48-hour veto window** to the last honest guardian.

3.

Mandatory Timelock: Recovery execution is delayed by a **mandatory 4-hour $\text{EXECUTION\_TIMELOCK}$** for final monitoring.
